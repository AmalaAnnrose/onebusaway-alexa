OneBusAway Alexa

An Java-based app to communicate with Amazon Alexa for devices such as the Amazon Echo

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Arrival information at your command

Say Alexa, where's my bus?, and quickly get real-time arrival information for your favorite bus stop.
